BAMRU's GOOGLE CALENDAR

How to Subscribe

1. Go to your own Google Calendar

2. Click the down-arrow next to Other calendars.

3. Select Add by URL from the menu.

4. The address for BAMRU's calendar is bamru.calendar@gmail.com.

5. Enter the address in the field provided.

6. Click Add calendar. The calendar will appear in the Other calendars section of the calendar list to the left.
